Temperature Conversion

One common application of functions is to convert between different units of measurement. Let's look at how to convert between Celsius and Fahrenheit temperatures.

Temperature Conversion Formula

To convert from Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the formula:

F = (C × 9/5) + 32

Where F is the temperature in Fahrenheit and C is the temperature in Celsius.
